Dish-n-Dash

The Ultimate Robotic Solution for Smart Waste Management in Schools

Our Mission

In school lunch settings, students often struggle to correctly sort their dishes and compostable materials. Our automated solution uses advanced robotics and computer vision to accurately sort waste, reducing contamination and improving recycling efficiency.

Key Features

Intelligent Vision

Advanced computer vision system using DETR with ResNet-50 backbone for accurate object detection

Robotic Control

Precise robotic manipulation using inverse kinematics for reliable waste sorting

Eco-Friendly

Optimizes recycling and composting while minimizing contamination
