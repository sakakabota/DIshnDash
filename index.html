Dish-n-Dash

Revolutionizing waste sorting in school cafeterias through robotics.

Dish-n-Dash is an innovative robotic system designed to streamline waste sorting and reduce contamination. Our project leverages state-of-the-art robotics and image classification technology to create a cleaner, greener future for schools.
