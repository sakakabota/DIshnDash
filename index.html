About Dish-n-Dash

Dish-n-Dash is a robotics project designed to automate waste sorting in cafeterias. Our system classifies and sorts waste into compost, recycling, or dish bins, reducing contamination and improving efficiency.

This project was developed as part of EECS 106A and integrates cutting-edge robotics and AI technology. Explore the sections below to learn about our pipeline, challenges, and future improvements.
