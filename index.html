Dish-n-Dash

Revolutionizing waste sorting in school cafeterias through robotics. Our automated system ensures accurate sorting, reduces contamination, and promotes eco-friendly habits among students.

Why Dish-n-Dash?

The Problem

Students often struggle with proper waste sorting, leading to contamination of recyclables and compost. This increases operational costs and hinders recycling processes.

Our Solution

An automated system that accurately classifies and sorts cafeteria waste, using advanced robotics and computer vision to ensure proper waste management.

The Impact

Reduced waste contamination, lower operational costs, and promotion of eco-friendly habits among students, supporting schools' environmental goals.
